About Me

I'm a first year Masters student at Carnegie Mellon University, majoring in Computer Science. My academic interests lie broadly in the field of Artificial Intelligence. I'm especially intrigued by its more practical aspects (such as Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision), and the interpretability of observations and inferences made on large networks and datasets. Consequently, I've also developed a liking for Social Network Analysis, as well as Graph Theory and Algorithms.

I hail from the South Indian metropolis of Chennai, where I spent a huge chunk of my life right from my pre-school days up to my undergrad times. Having obtained a B. Tech (Hons.) in Computer Science and Engineering from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Madras, I've now shifted to Pittsburgh, PA, in pursuit of a Masters degree here at CMU.
